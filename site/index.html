Questions before you book

How often should I have my windows professionally cleaned in Tucson?

Because of Tucson's desert climate and frequent dust storms, we recommend professional cleaning every 3 to 6 months for most homes. High-traffic areas or homes near construction may benefit from quarterly service. Our maintenance plans keep it consistent and affordable year-round.

Can you remove hard water stains from my windows?

Yes. Hard water staining is extremely common in Tucson. Our Top Tier package uses professional-grade treatments that safely restore clarity without scratching the glass, including on deposits homeowners assumed were permanent.

Do you clean windows during monsoon season?

We operate year-round, though we watch the weather closely. If severe weather is forecast, we reschedule for safety. Many customers prefer post-monsoon cleanings to clear the dust and debris summer storms leave behind.

Are your cleaning products safe for pets and children?

Yes. All our solutions are eco-friendly and biodegradable, safe for your family and pets, with no harm to the desert ecosystem.

How long does a typical appointment take?

For an average Tucson home (2,000 to 2,500 sq ft, 20 to 30 windows), our Tier 1 service takes 2 to 3 hours. Larger homes or more comprehensive packages may take longer; we'll give you a timeframe with your quote.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Not necessarily. For exterior-only service, you don't need to be home at all. For interior cleaning, we can arrange access that works for your schedule when you book.

How does booking work?

Submit the quote form (or email us) and you'll get a reply with real open appointment times. Reply with the one that works and it's booked, with a confirmation and calendar invite. No phone tag required.
