Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my windows professionally cleaned in Tucson?

Due to Tucson's desert climate and frequent dust storms, we recommend professional cleaning every 3–6 months for most homes. High-traffic areas or homes near construction may benefit from quarterly service. Our maintenance plans make it easy and affordable to stay consistently clean year-round.

Can you remove hard water stains from my windows?

Absolutely! Hard water staining is extremely common in Tucson. Our Top Tier package includes specialized removal using professional-grade treatments that safely restore clarity without scratching the glass. We've removed mineral deposits homeowners thought were permanent.

Do you clean windows during monsoon season?

Yes! We operate year-round, though we watch the weather closely. If severe weather is forecast, we'll reschedule to ensure safety and quality. Many customers actually prefer post-monsoon cleanings to clear the dust and debris summer storms leave behind.

Are your cleaning products safe for pets and children?

Yes. All our solutions are eco-friendly, biodegradable, and completely safe for your family and pets, with no harm to the desert ecosystem.

How long does a typical appointment take?

For an average Tucson home (2,000–2,500 sq ft, 20–30 windows), our Tier 1 service takes 2–3 hours. Larger homes or more comprehensive packages may take longer, and we'll give you a timeframe with your quote.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Not necessarily! For exterior-only service, you don't need to be home at all. For interior cleaning, we can arrange access that works for your schedule and sort out details when you book.

How does booking work?

Submit the quote form (or email us) and you'll get a reply with real open appointment times. Reply with the one that works and it's booked: you'll receive a confirmation with a calendar invite. No phone tag required.
