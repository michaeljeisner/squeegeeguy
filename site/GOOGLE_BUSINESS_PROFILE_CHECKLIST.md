# Google Business Profile Setup Checklist

## 1. Claim Your Listing
1. Go to https://business.google.com
2. Sign in with your Google account (use the business Google account, not personal)
3. Click "Add your business to Google"
4. Search for "SqueegeeGuy" — if it appears, click "Claim this business"
5. If not found, click "Add your business"

## 2. Core Info (complete all fields)
- [ ] **Business name:** SqueegeeGuy (exact — no keyword stuffing)
- [ ] **Primary category:** Window Cleaning Service
- [ ] **Additional categories:** Pressure Washing Service, Janitorial Service
- [ ] **Address:** Your physical business address (required for verification)
  - If home-based: use a PO box or UPS store address + enable "I deliver goods and services to customers" to hide the street address
- [ ] **Phone:** Your primary business number
- [ ] **Website:** https://squeegeeguy.com
- [ ] **Service area:** Add all Tucson zip codes you serve (85701–85756 range)
  - Alternatively: set a radius from your address (e.g., 25 miles)

## 3. Hours
- [ ] Set regular business hours
- [ ] Add special hours for holidays
- [ ] Enable "By appointment" if you don't have walk-ins

## 4. Description (750 chars max)
Write a natural description that includes your key services and location:
```
SqueegeeGuy is a locally owned window cleaning and pressure washing company serving Tucson, AZ businesses and homes. We specialize in commercial storefronts, restaurants, medical offices, car dealerships, and property management accounts. Licensed, insured, and committed to showing up on time with consistent results. Free quotes for all jobs.
```

## 5. Services
Add explicit service entries (Google uses these for local search):
- [ ] Window Cleaning (Commercial)
- [ ] Window Cleaning (Residential)
- [ ] Pressure Washing
- [ ] Power Washing
- [ ] Storefront Cleaning
- [ ] Parking Lot Cleaning
- [ ] Building Exterior Washing

## 6. Photos (minimum 10, aim for 20+)
Google ranks profiles with more photos higher.
- [ ] **Logo** (square, min 720x720px)
- [ ] **Cover photo** (landscape, 1080x608px) — action shot of you cleaning
- [ ] Before/after shots (at least 5 pairs)
- [ ] Team photo or solo photo of Michael
- [ ] Equipment photo
- [ ] Completed jobs (storefronts, restaurants, etc.)

Tip: name photo files descriptively before uploading:
`squeegee-guy-tucson-window-cleaning-restaurant.jpg`

## 7. Verification
Google requires verification to publish your listing:
- **Postcard (most common):** Google mails a code to your address within 5 days
- **Phone/email:** sometimes offered for new listings
- **Video verification:** newer option — record a walkthrough

## 8. After Verification: Get Reviews
Reviews are the #1 ranking factor for local search.
- [ ] Create a short review link: In GBP dashboard → "Get more reviews" → copy the link
- [ ] Text/email the link to every completed customer (first 5 reviews are critical)
- [ ] Add the link to your email signature and invoices
- [ ] Respond to EVERY review (positive and negative) within 48 hours

## 9. Posts (weekly, 5 minutes/week)
GBP posts appear in local search results and keep your profile active:
- Before/after photos with brief description
- Seasonal promotions ("Spring cleaning special for Tucson restaurants")
- Completed jobs ("Just finished a 12-window commercial job near U of A")

## 10. Q&A
Pre-populate the Q&A section:
- Q: "Do you offer free quotes?" A: "Yes! Contact us and we'll schedule a walkthrough."
- Q: "Are you licensed and insured?" A: "Yes, fully licensed and insured in Arizona."
- Q: "Do you do residential or just commercial?" A: "Both."
- Q: "What areas do you serve?" A: "All of Tucson and surrounding areas including Marana, Oro Valley, Sahuarita, and Green Valley."
